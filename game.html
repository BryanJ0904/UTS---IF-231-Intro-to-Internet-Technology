Gameplay Tips

1. Your pet has four status that must be kept an eye on.
   These include hunger, energy, happiness, and health. 
2. Reaching zero in happiness or health will result in the end of the game.
3. All status decrease every 5 seconds. The rate will be determined from the
   pets current level.
4. Your pet will level up every 40 seconds, each level will gradually get harder.
5. Your objective is to make sure everything is in balance by doing activities.
6. Your pet needs you, so better not leave it alone.
7. Don't let your pet get sick! If it does, don't forget to heal it.
8. CHOOSE THE ACTIVITIES WISELY, HAVE FUN PLAYING!
